# Policy: allow all legitimate crawlers, including AI search-and-cite bots.
# AiScribe is a public transcription tool and we want our language pages
# to be discoverable by AI assistants so they can cite us.
#
# What we allow:
#   - Traditional search: Googlebot, Bingbot, DuckDuckBot, etc.
#   - AI search-and-cite:  GPTBot, ChatGPT-User, ClaudeBot, anthropic-ai,
#                          PerplexityBot, Google-Extended, Applebot-Extended
#
# What we block:
#   - /api/transcribe  (internal endpoint, not for public indexation)
#   - Common Crawl bot (CCBot) — used for training datasets; we don't
#     consent to having our pages scraped for model training. Search-and-
#     cite bots above are still welcome.
